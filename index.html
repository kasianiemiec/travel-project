Why Corfu?

Corfu, the most lush of all the greek islands

Corfu, a gem of the Ionian Sea, enchants visitors with its lush green landscapes, crystal-clear waters, and charming Venetian architecture. The island’s rich history, influenced by Greek, Italian, and British cultures, creates a unique and vibrant atmosphere that captivates everyone who visits. From the picturesque old town to its golden beaches, Corfu is a paradise for nature and history lovers alike.

Beyond its stunning scenery, Corfu’s warm hospitality and relaxed Mediterranean lifestyle make it a dream destination. Whether indulging in fresh seafood by the sea, wandering through olive groves, or enjoying breathtaking sunsets, every moment on the island feels like pure magic.

Cafés

My favorite cafés in Corfu

Coconela Café

Coconela Cafe, nestled in the heart of Corfu Town, is a cozy spot known for serving some of the best-quality coffee in the city. Alongside its rich, aromatic brews, the café offers delicious home-baked goods made fresh on the spot.

Address:

Leoforos Alexandras 52, Corfu Town 49100 Greece

What i like about it:

One of the coolest places in town with a very interesting minimal design, the coffee is top quality and the food is delicious.

Anassa Arillas

Anassa Café in Arillas is a true seaside gem, offering a relaxed and welcoming atmosphere right on the waterfront. With cozy puffs and sofas set up on the beach, you can enjoy delicious fresh food, homemade cakes, and expertly brewed coffee.

Address:

Arillas Sea Front,
Corfu 49081 Greece

What i like about it:

The food is just breathtaking and the fact that you can eat it with your feet in the sand makes it for one of the best summer spots in North Corfu.

Old Buoy

Old Buoy Restaurant in Corfu Town is a charming spot by the port, offering a perfect blend of gourmet food, expertly crafted drinks, and rich, aromatic coffee. Its beautiful patio, creates a serene atmosphere for dining and unwinding.

Address:

Eleftheriou venizelou 28, Corfu, Kerkira, Greece 49100

What i like about it:

Love the food, love the vibe and the interior but most of all adore tha patio space where you can sit outside secluded from the hustle of the city.
